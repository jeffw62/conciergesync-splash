What is Onboarding?

Onboarding is the setup process that personalizes ConciergeSync™ for you. Think of it like your flight check-in — we confirm a few details, then you’re cleared for takeoff.

By completing onboarding, we’ll know which cards and loyalty programs you use, so we can show you the most valuable redemption options that are best for you.

Why It’s Necessary

Without onboarding, ConciergeSync™ would just be a blank console. With it, the platform becomes your personalized co-pilot — guiding you toward smarter decisions, better redemptions, and maximum travel value.

This step ensures you don’t see generic advice — everything is tailored to your profile, your cards, and your goals.
